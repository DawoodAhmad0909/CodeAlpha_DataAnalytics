## 📁 Projects

### 🕷️ Task 1 — Web Scraping
**Goal:** Extract real product data from Amazon fitness 
clothing section using Python.

- Scraped single and multiple product pages
- Collected 385 products across 7 pages
- Extracted: price, rating, reviews, fabric details
- Cleaned and structured data into CSV format

**Tools:** BeautifulSoup, Requests, Pandas

---

### 🔍 Task 2 — Exploratory Data Analysis
**Goal:** Explore and analyze Netflix Movies & TV Shows 
dataset to uncover key trends and patterns.

- Analyzed 8790 titles across 12 variables
- Handled missing values strategically
- Uncovered content distribution trends
- Identified Netflix growth patterns (2008-2021)

**Key Findings:**
- Netflix library is 70% Movies, 30% TV Shows
- USA and India dominate content production
- Content addition peaked in 2019
- Platform primarily targets adult audiences (TV-MA)

**Tools:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

---

### 📊 Task 3 — Data Visualization
**Goal:** Transform raw Netflix data into meaningful 
visual stories using professional charts.

**Charts Built:**
1. Pie Chart — Movies vs TV Shows split
2. Horizontal Bar — Top 10 content producing countries
3. Line Chart — Netflix content growth over years
4. Horizontal Bar — Top 10 genres on Netflix
5. Heatmap — Content added by year and type

**Tools:** Matplotlib, Seaborn

---

### 💬 Task 4 — Sentiment Analysis
**Goal:** Analyze customer sentiment from Amazon product 
reviews using Natural Language Processing.

- Extracted 1080 individual reviews from 224 products
- Classified reviews as Positive, Neutral or Negative
- Visualized sentiment distribution and polarity scores

**Key Findings:**
- 54% Positive, 42% Neutral, 4% Negative
- Overall customer sentiment is positive
- Mean polarity score confirms positive trend

**Tools:** TextBlob, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
